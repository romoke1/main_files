Our Vision Is For A World Where
No Girl Is Limited By Her Period

Our Mission

We aim to leverage on technology and an appeal to all human sense of decency and emphathy to ensure that no child experiences any form of stigma or prejudice whatsoever due to menstrual related challenges or prejudice whatsoever.

The Problem

Without the resources or ifrmation to manage menstruation, millions of girls around the world are forced to miss school during their periods.
This has serious personal and educational consequences, like falling behind in class, dropping out altogether and having to use unsafe substitutes for pads like mattress filling or leaves.
Luckily, we have a solution.
